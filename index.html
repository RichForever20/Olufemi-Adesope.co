OUR TOOLS & METHODOLOGIES FOR SUCCESS

We usually undertake consultancy assignments using innovative and data-based analytics to come to advisory decisions on specific issues leveraging on the knowledge and experience of our partners and consultants.

We deploy contemporary assessment and evaluation tools to arrive at professional judgements and opinions on optimization of landed property assets. These include the use of computer based applications and models.

Our standard operating guidelines are based on international best practices for property asset management. These equip our staff to meet clients’ expectations in terms of quality and efficiency of service delivery.

We maintain active collaboration with all stakeholders within the profession of Estate Surveying and Valuations to facilitate access to market information, intelligence and new thinking. Some of these stakeholders are the regulatory bodies in the building and allied industries and credible firms with local and international expertise in the profession.

Resumes Of Our Estate Surveyor and Valuer

At Olufemi Adesope & Co, our team of Estate Surveyors and Valuers (ESV) is composed of highly skilled and experienced professionals who bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every project.

Olufemi Adesope

Lead Consultant

He was educated at the University of Lagos and University of Ibadan where he obtained BSc (Estate Management) and MBA respectively. He is a registered Estate Surveyor and Valuer and worked for Cooperative Bank and Skye Bank Plc where he was in charge of property management and maintenance of all the banks’ landed properties across the nation.

He was also the unit head of the Property and Project management unit of the General Services department at Skye Bank Plc. Prior to working in the financial institutions; he worked with the professional firms of Funsho Oladimeji & Co and Epega & Co both in Lagos where he partook in valuation assignments for several private and public institution clients. Subsequently he worked with Wemabod Estates Limited and the Odua Investment Company Limited where he has responsibility for the management of cumulative property portfolios in excess of N70billion including iconic land mark buildings such as the Western House on Broad Street Lagos and Cocoa House in Ibadan. He is a Certified Facility Manager and a Class 23 Member of the Advance Management Programme of the Lagos Business School -2011

Adeyemi Ayetiran

Partner

He was educated at the University of Lagos where he obtained BSc (Estate Management) in 1992. He is a registered Estate Surveyor and Valuer and worked for The Federal Ministry of Works and Housing retiring as a Principal Lands Officer.

He is a trained facility management expert and also specializes in rural estate management and the valuation of antiquities. He has international exposure in managing housing projects in UK and has continually deployed the experience in attending to varying real estate development needs of individual and corporate clients in Nigeria and the Diaspora.

Olumide Oteniya

Partner

Mr Olumide Oteniya studied Estate management at the University of Lagos graduating in 1992. His professional career spans a 15 year stint with Jide Taiwo & Co rising to become the branch manager for Western Nigeria operations and an associate partner of the firm.

He has participated in the valuation of several assets across the nation for all types of clients including private individuals, Corporate Organizations and public institutions. He has attended many training and conferences in and outside the country.
