About Us

Olufemi Adesope & Co is a professional real estate consultancy firm registered with the Estate Surveyors & Valuers’ Registration Board of Nigeria.

With over 50 years partners' cumulative experience in the real estate industry, we are committed to helping our clients navigate the often complex and competitive market with confidence and ease.

At Olufemi Adesope & Company, we are more than just real estate agents; we are a one stop center for offering innovative services which create a brighter future for our clients. Our team of experienced professionals is passionate about providing sustainable solutions that meet their unique needs and aspirations.

We offer a comprehensive range of real estate services, including residential and commercial property sales, leasing, property & facility management, and investment consulting.

Our goal is to make your real estate decisions as optimal and stress-free as possible, delivering results that you can trust.
