About Us

Olufemi Adesope & Co is a professional real estate consultancy firm registered with the Estate Surveyors & Valuers’ Registration Board of Nigeria.

With years of experience in the real estate industry, our dedicated team is committed to helping you navigate the often complex and competitive market with confidence and ease.

At Olufemi Adesope & Co, we are more than just real estate agents; we are your partners in creating a brighter future. Our team of experienced professionals is passionate about connecting buyers and sellers, renters, and investors with properties that meet their unique needs and aspirations.

Whether you’re looking for a cozy starter home, a luxury estate, or a strategic investment property, we have the expertise and local market knowledge to make your real estate dreams a reality.

We offer a comprehensive range of real estate services, including residential and commercial property sales, leasing, property management, and investment consulting. Our goal is to make your real estate journey as smooth and stress-free as possible, delivering results that you can trust.
